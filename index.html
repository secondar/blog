Biography

As a nursing scholar and educator specializing in geriatric care, I am committed to integrating clinical practice, scientific evidence, and national policies to enhance the well-being of older adults. I earned my Master of Medicine degree from Nanjing University of Chinese Medicine in 2021. Supported by the National Natural Science Foundation of China and under the supervision of Professor Guihua Xu, I concentrated on utilizing interRAI tools for conducting geriatric health assessments in long-term care facilities and integrating evidence-based practices. Currently, I serve as a full-time lecturer at Wannan Medical College , where I have accumulated four years of experience teaching undergraduate nursing courses, mentoring students in research, and coordinating academic activities. My objective is to develop equitable, technology-driven models of aged care that can be applied globally.
